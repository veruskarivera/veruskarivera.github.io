Check In App


Design a product that can help people during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Identifying the Problem

"Re-entry Anxiety: fear about re-entering society and leaving quarantine behind.”

COVID-19 changed our lives in unexpected ways and since the lockdown restrictions
are beginning to be lifted, Americans are experiencing re-entry anxiety as they attempt
to resume regular activities and many don’t know where or how to start.

Proposing a Solution

One of the solutions to overcome anxiety is to focus on the things we can control.
Check-In, a reservation app, allows users to know what their preferred businesses are
doing to commit to CDC guidelines. It tracks real-time and expected occupancy. This will
not only help to avoid awkward situations but, help to gain the sense of control lost due
to the uncertainty of the pandemic.

EMPATHIZE

User Interview|User Research

Our target users range from ages 16 to 60+, who just want to know what’s going
on in the businesses they plan on visiting, without wasting time or encountering
awkward situations.

SCENARIO 1

Reserve

Amelia is browsing through the app and she
decided to make a reservation to go get her
nails done.

1. Login
2. Search and Click nail salon
3. Learn about safety measures
4. Write down required questions
by business
5. Reserve a time slot
6. Get QR code

FEEDBACK

"I found it easy to search for the nail salon I
wanted to go to. It made me feel comfortable
that I could see the COVID-19 precautions the
salon was taking before booking my appointment.
It gave me peace of mind knowing what to expect
upon arrival."

SCENARIO 2

Look For QR Code to Check In

Amelia is at the nail salon and she needs QR code
to Check-In.

1. Login
2. Open Menu
3. Look for Check In tab
4. Select Reservation
5. Show QR Code to business

FEEDBACK

"The app stores your reservations so it was easy
to pull up when I needed it. I would recommend
maybe giving notifications and alerts when it
gets sooner to your appointment time."
