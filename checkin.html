Check In App


Design a product that can help people during this unprecedented time

01

Identifying the Problem

Re-entry Anxiety: fear about re-entering society and leaving quarantine behind

COVID-19 changed our lives in unexpected ways and since the lockdown restrictions are getting lifted,
Americans are experiencing re-entry anxiety as they attempt to resume regular activities and many
don’t know where or how to start.

02

Proposing a Solution

One of the solutions to overcome anxiety is to focus on the things we can control. Check-In, a
reservation app, will allow users to know what their preferred business is doing to commit to
CDC guidelines and track real-time and expected occupancy. These will not only help them avoid
awkward situations but will also help them gain the sense of control they lost due to the uncertainty
of the pandemic.

03

Identifying the User

The user we are focusing on is people (from age 18 to 60+)
who just want to know what’s going on in the business they
want to visit without wasting time or encountering awkward situations or conflicts.

04

Create a Persona

Amelia is getting groceries at the supermarket and while
she’s there she realizes that there’s a lot of people not following
the guidelines. She starts to get uncomfortable when at the
cashier line people behind her are not respecting social
distancing, after a couple of minutes she decides to leave
without getting her groceries. Amelia wishes she knew that the
supermarket was busy and not forcing CDC guidelines.

05

Establish a Focus

Real-time capacity feature
Reservation feature
QR Code feature
